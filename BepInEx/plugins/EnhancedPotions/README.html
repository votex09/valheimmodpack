Enhanced Potions Mod for Valheim

Available on Nexus Mods and Thunderstore.io

This mod adds new powerful mead bases and potions. It provides a few tweaks in the gameplay for a mid-more-to-end game phase.

Potion List

All recipes are totally customizable. You can set the recipe requirements, item names and descriptions, duration and most of the effects as well. You can disable the ones that you don’t feel like using.

It currently adds 19 sets of mead base and potions. The default requirements are quite pricey, but it’s totally customizable.

- Megingjord’s extract

Gives the drinker a god’s strength

Duration 600s (10m)

Increased max carry weight, slower health regeneration.

- Bifröst’s apozem

Allows unrestricted Portal use

Duration 450s (7m 30s)

Portal usage without any restrictions, slower health and stamina regeneration

- Tyr’s decantation

Gives the power of flight

Duration 210s (3m 30s)

You can fly but can carry less weight

Instructions: Double-tap JUMP to activate/deactivate it. Press JUMP to go up and CROUCH to go down.

- Hrungnir’s effusion

Gives the power of breathing and walking underwater

Duration 600s (10m)

You can breathe and walk underwater, stamina regeneration is slower.

Instructions: You can recover stamina by staying put.

- Spyja solution

Smelling horrible and tasting even worse, it will make you vomit

Duration 20s (20s)

You feel sick, health and stamina regeneration is slower

Instructions: Clears the effects of all the consumed goods (food, potion and mead) while in effect and slows stats regeneration.

Restores stamina

Duration 300s (5m)

Stamina over time and increased stamina and health regeneration

- Greater Health solution

Restores health

Duration 300s (5m)

Health over time and increased health and stamina regeneration

Installation

  • Use Vortex Mod Manager

OR

  • Use Thunderstore Mod Manager

OR

  • Download and install BepInEx Valheim
  • Download and install Jotunn
  • Download this mod and move the zip contents so the <GameLocation>\BepInEx\plugins\EnhancedPotions directory looks like this:
    • Assets
    • DefaultEnhancedPotions.json
    • EnhancedPotions.dll
    • Newtonsoft.Json.dll
    • System.Data.dll
    • System.Runtime.Serialization.dll

Configuration

Run the game at least once with the mod enabled and then edit the <GameLocation>\BepInEx\config\hbocao.EnhancedPotions.json file.

ATTENTION: Do NOT change the MeadPrefabName and PotionPrefabName fields in the JSON. This will bring only pain and make Odin very disappointed.
